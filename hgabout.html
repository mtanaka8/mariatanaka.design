Hawaiian Graphics Rebranded

OVERVIEW

Hawaiian Graphics is a local store that offers a great amount of high-quality art supplies at a lower price. They offer membership discounts that help you save a lot more money. Their services include beginner and intermediate painting classes.

THE SKETCHING PROCESS

Throughout the sketching process, I was able to discover diffrent styles and shapes based on the ideas I've had to create the perfect logo for Doha.

CREATING THE LOGO

The logo consists of a fish with geometric patterns within. This concept focuses on Doha’s glass lanterns, they’re usually found in their shops. These glass lanterns are colorful and bright when they’re in use. The idea of the fish comes from the kites they fly in their city during their festivals. The kites are usually shaped as sea animals such as fish and sharks.
