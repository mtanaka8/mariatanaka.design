ELIXIR, The Lifesaver For Tattoos

CREATING THE BRAND

Elixir is a fictional tattoo-healing kit brand. It is offered for those who have recently gotten fresh new tattoos and in need of natural and safe products. The kit has everything you need for your tattoos to heal properly. The brand is bold and vibrant.

CREATING THE LOGO

The logo consists of a dragonfly. The dragonfly is known to be colorful and vibrant. The logo is symmetrical to give a balanced look. The dragonfly’s sharp tail resembles the end of a tattoo gun.

PATTERNS & COLORS

The colors for this brand are bright orange, pink, green and navy blue. They’re bright and bold and it goes along with the Elixir brand. The patterns consists of an baroque style with little graphics that you normally see at a tattoo parlor shop.
