THE BOOK TRILOGY

The book trilogy is three book covers based on three different podcasts but in the same categories. Since I chose Criminal podcasts, my book covers a lot more dark and grim horror-like stories. The purpose of this project is to listen to each podcast and to design a cover that represents the storyline. We needed to experiment with different mediums. I experimented with digital and traditional art.
