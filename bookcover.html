The Book Trilogy

OVERVIEW

The book trilogy is three book covers based on three different podcasts but in the same categories. Since I chose Criminal podcasts, my book covers a lot more dark and grim horror-like stories.

THE SKETCHING PROCESS

The purpose of this project is to listen to each podcast and to design a cover that represents the storyline. We needed to experiment with different mediums. I experimented with digital and traditional art.

CREATING THE COVERS

I was able to create one book cover for podcast and then I was able to create two more throughout the design process.
