DOHA TOURISM LOGO

We were instructed to design a tourism logo on a city of our choosing. I’ve selected Doha, a city located in Qatar. They’re known for their arabian coffee, tourist attractions and modern architecture. This project does not affiliate with the issue of harassment towards female migrant workers in Qatar's world cup hotels in 2022.
